Edit Cover Arts

Qoobar supports reading and writing of embedded pictures for all file types.

To add a picture to, or to change the picture of the selected files, click .

To remove the picture, click .

You can cut and copy a picture from one group of selected files and paste it into other files.

Tags importing from Discogs and Musicbrainz also imports cover art picture, if there is such.

You can edit cover art type and description in the panel shown below:

The Qoobar undo / redo system fully supports all cover art editing operations.

If you click on the picture, a dialog with full-size picture appears. To close it click on the picture again.

You can also resize the imported picture to desired width and height by clicking the button.

Exporting pictures to a hard drive is also supported. You can choose between JPG and PNG file formats.
